Welcome

Welcome to Outdoors Ireland, the go to site for those who love the outdoors life and a great resource for all that's going on in our beautiful island. To say Ireland has it all is an understatement! bustling cities, beaches, bogs and beautiful scenery, vast coastines where mauntains meet the sea, whilst the weather may not always be predictable and rain and wild conditions are the reason for its stunning and rugged landscapes.
The old saying of there is no such thing as bad weather but bad clothing is a statement all visitors should be advised on arrival to our beautiful Island, and it is an expression us adventurers certainly live by. Whether you are here for a visit or are looking for a day trip, weekend or evening activity you will find this site helpful and hopefully inspiring to get out and enjoy the world.
There are helpful links to sponor companies which can direct you to further web sites and for booking experiences. Look out on the Whats Going On for live updates as to the latest live and upcoming events so you dont miss out on the fun. You will also find offers and some of our partner websites may offer discounts when you mention Outdoors Ireland!!

About Ireland

Ireland according to my favourite source for facts Wikipedia is the 20th largest Island in the world with a population of over 7 million with the Republic having 5 million and Northern Ireland with a population of almost 2 million.
Needless to say a whole website could be dedicated to all the facts and history of the island however the main thing you need to know for this website is it is a relatively empty, medium sized island which means there are plenly opportunities to get out to nature, and you will never be too far from a beach, lake, maountain or village.
It is a safe place to travel and in recent years has become a relatively wealthy place which means good well serviced roads and infrastucture, and places to stay from the traditional irish B&B which are a feature in every town to all the major Hotel Chains and likewise for places to eat and drink you will be spoilt for choice.
